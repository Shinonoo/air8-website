AIR8 INDUSTRIES - SITE WIREFRAME NOTES (Day 1)
================================================

PAGES NEEDED:
1. Home (index.html)
   - Navbar: Home | About Us | Products | Contact
   - Hero section: banner image + tagline + CTA button ("Get a Quote")
   - Short company intro (from About Us)
   - Featured products preview (3 cards: Sound Attenuator, Vibration Isolators, Elta Fans)
   - Footer: address, phone, socials

2. About Us (about.html)
   - Vision statement
   - Mission statement
   - Company background / years of operation (fill in if available)

3. Products (products.html)
   - Grid of product cards linking to detail pages
   - Filter/category labels (Noise Control, Vibration Control, Fans & Blowers)

4. Product Detail Pages (dynamic or static per product)
   - abie-tiger-sound-attenuator.html
   - abie-tiger-vibration-isolators.html
   - elta-fans.html
   - Each: product image, description, specs, brochure download button, "Inquire about this product" button (pre-fills contact form)

5. Contact (contact.html)
   - Contact form: Name, Email, Phone, Product (dropdown), Message
   - Submits to POST /api/contact
   - On success -> redirect to thank-you.html
   - Company address, phone, embedded Google Map (optional)

6. Thank You (thank-you.html)
   - Confirmation message after form submission

DATABASE TABLES (Day 1 schema):
- inquiries: id, name, email, phone, product, message, created_at
- products: id, name, slug, category, description, brochure_url, image_url, created_at
