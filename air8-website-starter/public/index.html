About us

A dependable partner for air, sound and ductwork.

We specialize in three connected worlds: air movement through our partnership with Elta Fans, noise & vibration control through Abie Tiger, and ductwork & MEP accessories through Starduct. Our mission is simple — deliver high-quality products that meet the exact needs of each client, backed by real technical support.

From a single exhaust fan to a full building enclosure, we help you get the right solution the first time.

What we do

Three problems. One supplier.

AIR

Air movement & ventilation

Axial, centrifugal, roof, in-line and hazardous-area fans — plus blowers, controls and accessories for optimal airflow in any environment.

SOUND

Noise & vibration control

Silencers, acoustic louvers, sound-lock doors, barriers, enclosures and a full range of vibration isolators for noise-sensitive locations.

DUCT

Ductwork & MEP accessories

Spiral and rectangular ductwork, diffusers, dampers, VAV boxes and cable trays manufactured to AMCA, ASHRAE, UL and ISO standards.

FAQ

Common questions, answered.

Do you work with homeowners, or only companies?

Both. We supply single residential fans just as happily as full commercial packages — and we'll help you pick the right size either way.

How do I get a quotation?

Send the form below with what you need (a product name, a problem, or project plans). Our team reviews it and replies with a formal quotation.

Do you handle installation?

We're a dedicated supplier — installation is handled by your contractor. We support them with complete specifications, brochures and technical documentation.

Which areas do you serve?

We're based in Parañaque, Metro Manila, and deliver and support projects nationwide.

Which brands do you carry?

We're the authorized distributor of Elta Fans (Malaysia) for fans & blowers, Abie Tiger (Thailand) for acoustic solutions, and Starduct (Vietnam) for ductwork & MEP accessories.
